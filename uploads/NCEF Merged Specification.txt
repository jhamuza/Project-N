



NCEF Combined Specification (URS v1.7 & SDD v1.4)


1. Executive Summary


The NCEF is a new regulatory framework established by the Malaysian Communications and Multimedia Commission (MCMC) to modernize and regulate the registration of communications equipment in Malaysia. The development of a centralized online system is the cornerstone of this initiative, replacing legacy systems and manual workflows. This project is aligned with MCMC’s and the nation’s digital transformation goals to enhance public service delivery through efficient, secure, and intelligent technology.


2. System Objectives


The primary objectives of the NCEF system design are to: - Streamline Operations: Design an efficient, automated, and user-centric platform that simplifies the end-to-end registration process for all stakeholders. - Enhance Regulatory Oversight: Architect a system with robust data management, audit trails, and reporting capabilities to provide MCMC with comprehensive oversight of the equipment lifecycle. - Improve Service Delivery: Create a transparent, accessible, and responsive system that improves the user experience for applicants and the public. - Integrate Seamlessly: Design a well-defined integration architecture to connect with critical internal (MCMC SIFS) and external (SIRIM, RMCD, SSM, MCMC Pay) systems. - Leverage AI: Incorporate Artificial Intelligence to enhance efficiency and accuracy in document validation, risk assessment, and decision support. - Ensure Security and Compliance: Build a secure, scalable, and reliable platform that complies with the Malaysian Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) 2010 and best-in-class security standards.


3. System Architecture


The NCEF Online Registration System is a comprehensive, web-based platform designed to manage the entire lifecycle of communications equipment registration in Malaysia.


3.1 Logical Architecture


The system is composed of several key portals and applications: - Internal Portal: A secure portal for MCMC officers (System Administrators, OICs, Recommenders, Verifiers, Approvers) to manage applications, configure system settings, monitor operations via dashboards, and perform all back-office functions. - External Portal: A self-service portal for applicants (Suppliers, Principals, Consultants) to register accounts, submit and manage equipment registration applications (SDoC), special approval, renewals, and modifications. It includes a personalized dashboard for tracking application status and managing payments. - Public Portal: An open-access portal for the public to search and verify the certification status of equipment, access regulatory documents (guidelines, standards), view FAQs, and interact with an AI-powered chatbot. - Mobile Applications: Native mobile apps (Android, iOS, Huawei) providing key functionalities on the go, including public search and push notifications for MCMC announcements.

6. Functional Modules


6.1 Equipment Registration Module (SDoC)


The Equipment Registration Module is the core component for managing the Supplier’s Declaration of Conformity (SDoC). The system provides different workflows based on the risk level of the equipment, categorized into Scheme A, Scheme B, and Scheme C.


6.2 Special Approval Module


The Special Approval module provides a dedicated workflow for the importation of equipment that does not fall under standard SDoC registration. This includes Low/Medium Risk, High Risk, and Prohibited equipment.


6.3 Post-Market Surveillance (PMS) Module


Source: SDD Section 5.8 The PMS module enables MCMC to monitor and track equipment compliance after registration. It includes functionalities for scheduling surveillance activities, tracking compliance status, and managing enforcement actions.


6.4 Complaint & Surveillance Module


Source: SDD Section 5.9 This module handles complaints from the public or industry regarding non-compliant equipment. It provides workflows for investigation, escalation, and resolution tracking.


6.5 Compliance Status Management Module


Source: SDD Section 5.10 Manages the overall compliance status of registered equipment and suppliers, linking with the PMS and Complaint modules to provide a holistic view of compliance.


6.6 Public Module & Mobile Application


Source: SDD Sections 5.10 & 5.11 Provides an open-access portal and native mobile applications (Android, iOS, Huawei) for the public to search and verify equipment certification status, access regulatory documents, and receive push notifications.


7. AI-Enabled Features


The system incorporates Artificial Intelligence to enhance efficiency:


AI Document Validation: Automatically extracts and validates information from uploaded documents using Intelligent Character Recognition (ICR).


Risk Scoring & Assessment: Analyzes application data against historical patterns to assign a risk score.


Auto-Acceptance Thresholds (Scheme C): Applications with a confidence score >90% are automatically accepted.


Fraud Detection: Identifies potential fraudulent or fake applications.


AI Chatbot/Assistant: Provides instant support on the public portal.
